# Campaign State

## Story So Far

The party was imprisoned in Vaultspire, a massive black basalt fortress in the southern desert. Szeth and Alarak were held on floor 50 (convicted mass murderers); Rayne, Vacir, and Kaelen were on floor 13 (simpler crimes). Prisoners worked the Black Pits mines and blacksmithed at the forge to meet daily quotas - miss the quota and you lose cafeteria access. Everyone bonded over salad and mystery meat slosh and began devising a breakout plan.

Over several sessions inside the prison, the party gathered fork prongs to use as lockpicks. Grimshaw, an old half-elf prisoner, gave his mining quota to Alarak so he could eat. Vacir befriended Helga, the mess hall lunch lady. Szeth went trash can diving for a lost lockpick kit, recovering 2 prongs. Alarak got into it with a guard and forfeited meals two days running - Szeth smuggled him bricked beans. Vacir attempted to pick his cell lock and failed, breaking a prong. After Rayne joined the planning, the team accumulated enough prongs and hatched a night escape: pick the cell locks, sneak past the guard, hide in the weekly supply crates bound for Ironwood Fortress.

On escape night, Vacir, Slick, and Rayne picked their locks and crept past the dozing guard. Slick and Rayne headed for the mess hall entrance while Vacir climbed to floor 50 to free Szeth and Alarak, who had failed to break out on their own. One prong was used as a distraction to slip past the guard quarters on the way back down, leaving only one prong. Slick and Rayne fell over three times trying to enter an access vent - just as a mysterious figure approached from above. Vacir recognized her: Helga.

Helga retrieved her savings (a heavy chest, for her sick mother), declared she was out of PTO, and joined the escape. She unlocked the mess hall, distracted the guards, and threw flour in a suspicious guard's eyes while Vacir, Alarak, and Szeth disguised themselves as cafeteria workers to recover her. Everyone - including Helga and her savings - was hidden inside storage crates bound for Ironwood Fortress. The carriage left Vaultspire and the party leveled up to level 2. Rayne scouted ahead as a rat; rough terrain knocked her off the carriage and Slick summoned Beef the hawk to catch her mid-air. Beef immediately eyed her hungrily as she shifted back to human form.

The crates were delivered to a soup kitchen's storage room in Ironwood Fortress. There the party reunited with Bristle, Rayne's sassy flying squirrel companion, who had a peculiar drawing strapped to his leg - a short stout man in shiny armor. Slick tried to solve a voice-activated lock mechanism, failed, argued with it, and tripped the alarm. Szeth blasted it. Rayne, Vacir, and Alarak escaped through the hole Bristle indicated; Szeth, Slick, and Helga were spotted by guards. A wooden beam snapped under Helga's dash and ended the fight. Helga toppled onto Vacir in the chaos - their first accidental kiss. Bristle gave the reunited party a few frustrated squeaks about their performance.

Bristle led the party to his stash where they picked up equipment and weapons. Everyone received armor except Vacir, who is still in prison clothing. Rayne revealed lore about a special artifact she has a strong connection to - before her arrest at Lord Halvar Dendros's home, she buried it under an Oak tree. Bristle had been watching over it and reported that someone matching Lord Halvar Dendros's description came to unearth it. Bristle also spotted a Dendros look-alike among guards on their lunch break.

Vacir snuck in to investigate and decapitated the first person he encountered - Cindy, an innocent soup kitchen worker, not a guard. He brought the body back but left the head, alerting the guards. In the ensuing chaos, Slick was pierced by two arrows and went into death saving throws without Mage Armor up. Rayne wildshaping as a bear tried to drag him to safety through a window but accidentally bit him, adding more failed saves. Kaelen died. Helga was left behind in the chaos - stuck inside the soup kitchen as the party fled, yelling at them not to leave her. The party fled into the forest, with Rayne ditching Slick's body in a dumpster after shifting back to human form.

The party took a long rest in the forest. During the rest, Szeth was visited by Inkus in a dream - his Warlock patron, with whom he has a contract requiring him to recruit members into Inkus's religion. Bristle woke everyone by biting their toes and reported that a feast was happening that night at the Silver Oak in Ironwood Fortress, with Lord Halvar Dendros (or his look-alike) in attendance. Bristle also spotted Helga back at work in the soup kitchen.

While out searching for clothes to steal, the party came across a Thornecrest carriage. Darian Thornecrest had been tricked by thieves and lost all his gold and possessions. The party showed him Kaelen's body. Darian said the Thornecrest family prayer over his brother and agreed to join the crew to find the Bag of Holding.

Bristle informed the group that Alarak had slipped away in the night, leaving behind a letter: he would take responsibility for returning Helga's chest to her family, and was parting ways due to the party's overly chaotic tendencies. The party drove to the Silver Oak in Darian's Thornecrest carriage and met Briggs, a large orc guard at the gate. Darian mistook him for a valet; Szeth paid him 5gp to park the carriage and keep the peace. The party split - Darian and Szeth entered through the front door to join the feast, while Vacir, Rayne, and Bristle climbed vines to the second floor and slipped in through a window.

Inside, the Lord Halvard Dendros look-alike took the podium and gave a speech naming the 5 wanted rebels. Slick was marked with a red X. Darian stepped forward to confront the speaker for dishonoring his brother's name; after being ignored, Szeth tried to pull him back - and Darian pointed out that Szeth was the man on the wanted poster. The guards realized criminals were present and combat broke out. Briggs set fire to distract the guards; Vacir fought; Bristle and Rayne held off Dendros. Szeth and Darian were overwhelmed in melee and both went down. Briggs dragged Szeth out to the carriage; Bear Rayne grabbed Vacir in her mouth and dashed out to join them. Rayne healed Szeth and Vacir while Briggs drove the carriage away. Darian was killed in the battle - the last Thornecrest brother fell at the Silver Oak.
