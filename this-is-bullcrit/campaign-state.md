# Campaign State

## Story So Far

The party escaped from Vaultspire prison by striking a deal with Helga, a lunch lady at the mess hall. They smuggled items for her to her family in exchange for her silence, hiding in food crates that were loaded onto a wagon and delivered to a soup kitchen in Ironwood Town.

Bristle, Rayne's flying squirrel companion, led the party to his stash where they picked up equipment and weapons. Everyone received armor except Vacir, who is still in prison clothing. Rayne revealed lore about a special artifact she has a strong connection to - before her arrest at Lord Halvar Dendros's home, she buried it under an Oak tree. Bristle had been watching over it and reported that someone matching Lord Halvar Dendros's description came to unearth it. Bristle also spotted a Dendros look-alike among guards on their lunch break.

Vacir snuck in to investigate and decapitated the first person he encountered - Cindy, an innocent soup kitchen worker, not a guard. He brought the body back but left the head, alerting the guards. In the ensuing chaos, Slick was pierced by two arrows and went into death saving throws without Mage Armor up. Rayne wildshaping as a bear tried to drag him to safety through a window but accidentally bit him, adding more failed saves. Kaelen died. The party fled into the forest, with Rayne ditching Slick's body in a dumpster after shifting back to human form.

The party took a long rest in the forest. During the rest, Szeth was visited by Incus in a dream - a fiend with whom he has a contract requiring him to recruit members into Incus's religion. Bristle woke everyone by biting their toes and reported that a feast was happening that night at the Silver Oak in Ironwood Town, with Lord Halvar Dendros (or his look-alike) in attendance. Bristle also spotted Helga back at work in the soup kitchen.

While out searching for clothes to steal, the party came across a Thornecrest carriage. Darian Thornecrest had been tricked by thieves and lost all his gold and possessions. The party showed him Kaelen's body. Darian said the Thornecrest family prayer over his brother and agreed to join the crew to find the Bag of Holding.

## Loose Threads
