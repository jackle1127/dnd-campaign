# Campaign State

## Story So Far

The party escaped from Vaultspire prison by striking a deal with Helga, a lunch lady at the mess hall. They smuggled items for her to her family in exchange for her silence, hiding in food crates that were loaded onto a wagon and delivered to a soup kitchen in Ironwood Town.

In Ironwood Town, they tripped an alarm and guards flooded in. Kaelen "Slick" Thornecrest (jackle1127's first character) was shot and went down - he didn't have Mage Armor up. Rayne wildshaping as a bear tried to carry him to safety but accidentally bit him, giving him extra failed death saves. Kaelen died. Helga escaped through a 10ft high window and made it out with the party.
