# Campaign State

## Story So Far

The party escaped from Vaultspire prison by striking a deal with Helga, a lunch lady at the mess hall. They smuggled items for her to her family in exchange for her silence, hiding in food crates that were loaded onto a wagon and delivered to a soup kitchen in Ironwood Town.

At the soup kitchen, the party received equipment and weapons from Bristle, Rayne's flying squirrel companion (Vacir and Slick did not receive any). Rayne revealed lore about a special artifact she has a strong connection to - before her arrest at Lord Halvar Dendros's home, she buried it under an Oak tree. Bristle had been watching over it and reported that someone matching Lord Halvar Dendros's description came to unearth it. Bristle also spotted a Dendros look-alike among guards on their lunch break.

Vacir snuck in to investigate and decapitated the first person he encountered - Cindy, an innocent soup kitchen worker, not a guard. He brought the body back but left the head, alerting the guards. In the ensuing chaos, Slick was pierced by two arrows and went into death saving throws without Mage Armor up. Rayne wildshaping as a bear tried to drag him to safety through a window but accidentally bit him, adding more failed saves. Kaelen died. Helga escaped through a 10ft high window and made it out with the party.

## Loose Threads

- Rayne's artifact: last seen being unearthed by Lord Halvar Dendros (or his look-alike) near the Oak tree where Rayne buried it
- Lord Halvar Dendros look-alike spotted among Ironwood Town guards - unknown significance
- Helga's family: the party still owes her a delivery
