# Grimshaw

**Type:** Half-elf  
**Affiliation:** Vaultspire prison (fellow prisoner)  
**Status:** Unknown - last seen in Vaultspire  

## Overview

Grimshaw is an old half-elf who was imprisoned in Vaultspire on floor 13 alongside Rayne, Vacir, and Kaelen. He showed kindness to Alarak by giving him his mining quota so that Alarak could eat when he had forfeited his own meal.

## Known Information

- Imprisoned on floor 13 of Vaultspire
- Gave his mining quota to Alarak so Alarak could access the cafeteria
- Fate unknown - was not part of the escape
