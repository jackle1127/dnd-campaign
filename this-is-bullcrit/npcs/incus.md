# Incus

**Type:** Fiend (likely incubus)
**Affiliation:** Unknown - connected to Szeth Stormblessed
**Status:** Active

## Overview

Incus is a fiend who visits Szeth in his dreams. The nature of their connection is unclear - possibly Szeth's Warlock patron or a fiend with a vested interest in him.

## Contract with Szeth

Incus has a contract with Szeth that requires Szeth to recruit more members into his religion. The terms and consequences of this contract are unknown.

## Known Information

- Visited Szeth during a dream while the party took a long rest in the forest outside Ironwood Town (session 2026-04-10)

## Notes

No official D&D source covers a fiend named Incus - likely a campaign-original character. Incubi in D&D are shapeshifting fiends that serve demons, devils, and other powerful entities, often acting as spies, manipulators, and dream-visitors.
