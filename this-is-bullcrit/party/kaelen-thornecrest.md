## Backstory

Kaelen Thornecrest was born the youngest son of House Thornecrest, an old and wealthy noble family whose sprawling estate lies in Bramblewood, a day's ride north of Bramblewood. The Thornecrests are known for their merchant fleets, political connections, and carefully curated reputation among Bramblewood's elite.

Kaelen grew up surrounded by opulence but overshadowed by his siblings. His older brother, Darian, was groomed to inherit the family fortune and prestige. His sister, Liora, turned away from wealth and status altogether, leaving to become a monk. That left Kaelen - brilliant, spoiled, and desperate to prove himself in a house that never seemed to need him.

Recognizing his natural talent for magic, his parents enrolled him in the Arcanum Lyceum in Bramblewood, one of Faerûn's most esteemed wizarding academies. Kaelen excelled academically, mastering the fundamentals of the arcane faster than most, but his arrogance quickly alienated peers and professors alike. He flaunted his intellect, mocked the less gifted, and bent rules to suit himself.

His downfall was spectacularly petty. Caught using Mage Hand to steal food from the academy cafeteria, Kaelen tried to charm his way out of trouble, then attempted to erase a guard's memory - and instead set the kitchen ablaze with a miscast Fire Bolt. The Lyceum expelled him the next morning.

When word reached Bramblewood, his family's fury was swift and public. To preserve their honor, they disowned him, striking his name from the family ledger. But Kaelen didn't leave empty-handed. Before departing, he broke into his father's study and stole the family's Bag of Holding, filled with gold and documents revealing House Thornecrest's secret dealings with The Argent Circle - a clandestine alliance of nobles, merchants, and mages who quietly manipulate the politics of Bramblewood and beyond.

Fleeing south, Kaelen found his way to Sea Mist, a vast coastal metropolis where commerce, corruption, and ambition thrive in equal measure. There, he abandoned his title and name, becoming simply "Slick."

He used his magic to cheat at games, forge documents, and con merchants - eventually catching the attention of the Wet Bandits, a small-time but clever gang that specialized in high-end thefts and magical smuggling. Slick's illusions and quick wit made him indispensable, and for a time, he thrived in the city's underbelly.

But his ambition never dulled. He began cutting side deals with the Waterfront Triad and trading information to the Xanathar Syndicate, hoping to climb higher than his old gang ever could. When the Argent Circle discovered their secrets were circulating through Sea Mist's black markets, they made sure someone paid the price.

During a late-night job on the docks, the Sea Mist City Watch ambushed the Wet Bandits. As guards flooded the warehouse, anti-magic runes flared to life beneath Slick's feet, draining his power before he could react. Shackled in rune-bound cuffs, he was taken down in seconds.

Within days, he was shipped north to Vaultspire - a massive, fortified city built for one purpose: to contain and process prisoners. A place where even magic bows to law.

## Family of House Thornecrest

### Lord Garran Thornecrest - The Patriarch
Cold, calculating head of House Thornecrest. A master merchant and politician who values reputation above all else. He disowned Kaelen without hesitation and likely orchestrated his imprisonment to protect the family's image.

### Lady Merisse Thornecrest - The Matriarch
Elegant, social, and fiercely protective of appearances. Deeply ashamed of Kaelen's scandal but quietly heartbroken. Pretends he doesn't exist in public, yet secretly keeps tabs on him through hired agents.

### Darian Thornecrest - The Heir
The perfect noble son - disciplined, charming, and politically savvy. Loyal to the family's ambitions and sees Kaelen as a failure who deserved exile. Secretly envies his brother's magical talent and boldness.

### Liora Thornecrest - The Exile
A peaceful, introspective monk who left nobility behind. Still cares deeply for both her brothers, especially Kaelen. Represents the family's conscience - detached from power, but not from love.

### Kaelen "Slick" Thornecrest - The Outcast
The youngest and most brilliant - but arrogant, reckless, and prideful. Disowned after his magical scandal at the Arcanum Lyceum. Now a clever, streetwise wizard plotting his comeback from the depths of Vaultspire.

## Timeline

### Early Life
Year 0-10: Born the youngest of three to the wealthy and influential House Thornecrest. Grows up spoiled and competitive in the shadow of his older siblings.

Year 11-16: Discovers an exceptional talent for magic; tutors call him a prodigy. Enrolled at Arcanum Lyceum in Bramblewood.

### Arcanum Lyceum
Year 17-20: Rises quickly through his studies, mastering spells years ahead of his peers. Gains a reputation as both brilliant and insufferable.

Year 20 (The Incident): Caught using Mage Hand to steal from the academy cafeteria. Tries to erase a guard's memory, fails, and accidentally sets the kitchen on fire. Immediately expelled.

### Exile from House Thornecrest
Year 20: News of his expulsion reaches the Thornecrest estate. His family publicly disowns him. Before leaving, Kaelen steals his father's Bag of Holding, which contains a fortune in gold and sensitive documents linking House Thornecrest to the Faerûn Mafia.

### Life in the Underworld
Year 21-23: Reinvents himself as "Slick." Recruited into the Wet Bandits. Becomes the crew's magical specialist.

Year 23-24: Begins cutting side deals with the Waterfront Triad and Xanathar Syndicate. When the Faerûn Mafia learns their stolen documents are circulating, they place a bounty on Slick's head.

### Capture and Imprisonment
Year 24: During a botched heist, the City Watch raids the hideout. Slick is arrested, his Bag of Holding confiscated. Shipped to Vaultspire.

## Love Interest

During his early years in Sea Mist, Kaelen met a young woman named Elyra Vance, a streetwise baker's daughter with a sharp tongue and a kind heart. She was one of the few people who saw past his arrogance and treated him like a person, not a failure or a project.

She'd often bring him fresh quiche - her signature dish - joking that it was "fit for a prince who fell off his throne." It became his comfort food, and her company the one thing that reminded him what being cared for actually felt like.

## The Crime He Was Never Caught For

When Kaelen first arrived in Sea Mist, he survived by pickpocketing drunk patrons in a dockside tavern. Using nothing but timing and a careful Mage Hand, he lifted coins and rings with ease - until Elyra caught him mid-theft.

She was tending bar for her father and grabbed his wrist without raising her voice or calling the Watch. Instead of exposing him, she quietly made him return what he'd taken, then fed him a slice of her homemade quiche before sending him on his way.

It was the only crime Kaelen ever committed that left him poorer in coin - but richer in consequence.
