## Level-by-Level Plan

### Level 3 - College of Lore
**Subclass:** College of Lore (obvious fit for the scholarly wanderer)  
**Cutting Words:** Reaction - subtract your Bardic Inspiration die from an enemy's attack roll, damage roll, or ability check. Best used to flip a near-miss into a miss, or to gut a big damage roll.  
**Bonus Proficiencies:** Pick 3 skills. Good options: Insight, Stealth, Sleight of Hand (Fib already has the social and knowledge skills covered)  
**New Spell pick:** Faerie Fire (no concentration issue at this level, gives party advantage on attacks - excellent) or Sleep (still works on low-HP enemies)

### Level 4 - First ASI
**Option A (recommended):** Fey Touched feat - +1 CHA (now 16), learn Misty Step + one 1st-level divination/enchantment spell (Silvery Barbs is S-tier) for free, both usable with CHA  
**Option B:** ASI +2 CHA → 16 straight. Safe, clean.  
**Option C:** War Caster - advantage on CON saves for concentration. Good if you plan to hold Hypnotic Pattern.  
**New cantrip:** Starry Wisp (scales with level, hits at range) or Mage Hand

### Level 5 - Font of Inspiration
Font of Inspiration: Bardic Inspiration recharges on **Short Rest** now. This is massive - you go from a once-per-long-rest resource to something you can freely spend.  
d8 Inspiration die.  
**New Spell picks (L3 slots unlock):** Hypnotic Pattern (best control spell in the game, concentration), Fear (mass frighten, concentration), Dispel Magic (always have this)

### Level 6 - Magical Discoveries
Pick 2 spells from the Cleric, Druid, or Wizard lists. You can swap these at each level-up.  
**Top picks:**
- **Fireball** (Wizard L3) - AoE you otherwise can't get. Big damage filler.
- **Spiritual Weapon** (Cleric L2) - Bonus action damage, no concentration. Frees your action for Cutting Words/Bardic Inspiration.
- **Web** (Wizard L2) - Restrains, no concentration, great area denial.
- **Pass Without Trace** (Druid L2) - +10 to stealth checks for the whole party. Situationally broken.
- **Slow** (Wizard L3) - Halves enemy movement, -2 AC, negates reactions. No save = no problem.

Recommended for Fib: **Spiritual Weapon + Fireball** (gives him a damage floor he currently lacks)

### Level 7 - Countercharm
Concentration action to give nearby allies advantage vs. Charmed/Frightened. Niche but free.  
**New Spell (L4 slots):** Greater Invisibility (attack with advantage while invisible, concentration - huge), Polymorph

### Level 8 - Second ASI
ASI +2 CHA → 18 (or 20 if you took Fey Touched at 4, which gave +1 already)

### Level 9 - Second Expertise
Pick 2 more skills to double proficiency on. Good choices: Deception, Arcana (Fib's knowledge skills)  
**New Spell (L5 slots):** Synaptic Static (4d6 psychic + -1d6 to rolls for concentration-free), Mass Suggestion (6 creatures, no concentration - devastating)

### Level 10 - Magical Secrets + d10
Pick **any 2 spells** from any class list. No restriction.  
**Top Magical Secrets picks:**
- **Summon Fey** (Druid L3) - persistent summon, great action economy
- **Counterspell** (Wizard L3) - always worth having
- **Wall of Force** (Wizard L5) - literally uncounterable battlefield control
- **Steel Wind Strike** (Ranger L5) - 6d10 to 5 targets, no concentration
- **Animate Objects** (Wizard L5) - insane damage output

Recommended: **Counterspell + Summon Fey** (or Steel Wind Strike for more offense)

## Spell Selection Reference

### Keep Forever
- Healing Word (bonus action revive, never drop this)
- Vicious Mockery (reliable debuff cantrip)
- Hypnotic Pattern (best control spell, level 5+)
- Dispel Magic

### Consider Swapping Out as Levels Progress
- Charm Person → swap at level 5 for something with more late-game value
- Thunderwave → swap at level 7 for Shatter or keep if melee pressure is frequent
- Dissonant Whispers → solid but replaceable at higher levels

### Fib-Specific Picks to Build Around
- Find Familiar (already have via Magic Initiate) - use Owl for Help action, gives party advantage on attacks
- Minor Illusion + Expertise Persuasion = incredible roleplay toolkit
- Faerie Fire + Cutting Words = shut down enemy offense completely
